About PCB AR

An augmented reality assistant that guides technicians to repair circuit boards instead of discarding them, keeping electronics in service and cutting e-waste.

62 Mte-waste / year
22%recycled
$62Bmaterials lost

Every repaired board avoids roughly 0.4 kg of e-waste and 12 kg of CO₂ versus manufacturing a replacement, directly supporting SDG 12.5 (substantially reduce waste through reuse) and SDG 9 (resilient industry).

How it works

1. Print the marker. Tap the button below, then print it on plain paper. Make it big (about 8 to 10 cm), keep the white border, and place it flat on or beside the board.

🖨️ Open marker to print
  1. Tap Start and allow the camera.
  2. Scan the ArUco marker and point at the board.
  3. Tap the round point to see component info.
  4. Tap 🔧 Start Diagnosis and check each component.
  5. Drag to rotate, pinch to zoom.

Tip: bright, even light and a steady hand detect best.
