How it works

  1. Tap Start and allow the camera.
  2. Print the ArUco marker (see README) and place it on/near the board.
  3. Point at the board — wait for Board Locked.
  4. Tap a glowing component to inspect its fault.
  5. Tap Start Diagnosis and answer the questions.
  6. Open 📊 to see your e-waste impact.
